The day one image brought my server down

During the viral expansion of the “Richmond news paper editorial fiasco”, I submitted a post on the road.cc forum. In it, I had added an image linked straight from my server… What a great idea! Linking one of the most read cycling forum in the UK to my own domain! Lots of hits! … yeah, just that.

I definitely need to do some memory tweaking there, because that single image, requested by the road.cc page views, brought my server to its knees. Not – good.
